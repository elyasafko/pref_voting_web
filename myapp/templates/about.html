About

This demo lets you explore two strategic-voting manipulation algorithms: C-MaNego (single manipulator) and CC-MaNego (coalition of k).

Enter a team profile, opponent ranking, number of manipulators, and a positional-scoring rule to see if—and how—the manipulators can force their preferred outcome.
